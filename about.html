About Our Chamber

Located in the heart of Pokhara, Aabiskar Law Chamber is dedicated to upholding the rule of law. Led by experienced advocates, we provide nuanced legal advice tailored to the unique socio-legal landscape of Nepal.

In the pursuit of justice, our firm navigates the complexities of the Civil Code 2074 and the Criminal Code of Nepal with precision. We specialize in constitutional advocacy, ensuring that the fundamental rights of our clients are protected under the spirit of the 2072 Constitution.

Our firm history dates back over a decade when our founding partners recognized a gap in high-end legal consultancy in the Western region. Since then, we have evolved into a full-service chamber, employing advanced legal research methodologies and maintaining a comprehensive library of Nepalese jurisprudence.

We engage in proactive litigation, which means we don't just react to legal threats—we anticipate them. Our corporate clients benefit from our risk-assessment audits, while our individual clients appreciate our empathetic approach to sensitive family matters.

We believe that every client deserves high-quality representation characterized by integrity, transparency, and deep legal scholarship.

Our Legal Philosophy

The law is a living instrument. In Nepal, where traditional values meet modern legislative reforms, Aabiskar Law Chamber serves as the bridge. We adhere to the principles of natural justice and ensure that administrative law is applied fairly to all citizens of Pokhara and Kaski.

Our advocates are regular contributors to legal journals and participate in bar association seminars to stay ahead of new precedents set by the Supreme Court of Nepal. This commitment to continuous learning is what makes us the best in the region.
