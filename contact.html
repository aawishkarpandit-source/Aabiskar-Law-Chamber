Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for a consultation?

Fees depend on the complexity of the case. We offer fixed rates for basic consultations and document reviews.

How long does a land dispute case take in Pokhara?

While we strive for fast resolution, court proceedings can vary. We offer mediation to speed up the process.

Do you handle cases outside of Kaski district?

Yes, our advocates represent clients in the High Courts of other provinces and the Supreme Court in Kathmandu.

Do I need to bring documents for the first meeting?

It is highly recommended to bring any relevant documents, such as citizenship certificates, land ownership proofs (Lalpurja), or court notices, to help us understand your case better.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we can arrange consultations via Zoom or WhatsApp for clients who are unable to visit our Pokhara office in person.
